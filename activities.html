ACTIVITIES

As well as a full racing calendar, featuring the Porsche Sports Cup, DTM series, ADAC Truck Racing, and the Nürburgring Drift Cup, the Nürburgring has a plathora of other activities for fans to enjoy. Please click the activity images below for more information on each.

Tourist Drives

The Nürburgring. The Green Hell. Rapid pulse, racing heart, adrenaline rush. Experience the legendary track first hand through your own visor. In a car or with your motorbike. Enjoy the tourist drives on the Nordschleife or the Grand Prix Track and put your tyres on the legendary asphalt. Become one with each corner and straight – and with the myth of the Nürburgring.

Copilot Experience

Experience the thrill of the Nürburgring like never before. Strap in and hold on tight as you are driven around the Nordschleife at speeds you wouldn't believe. Our experienced racing drivers show you how the Pros do it in one of our Mercedes AMG GT R Pro cars.

Nürburgring Driving Academy

Want to master the Nürburgring? We can teach you how. Learn under the tutelage of professional instructors to learn everything there is to know about driving the Nordschleife. If that's not enough for you, we also have training to achieve a national A license to take part in professional race on the Grand Prix circuit!

Entertainment

The Nürburgring hosts live events all year round. Choose from live football on our 45x8m LED ring wall, BigFM party nights with live DJs and bands, German league championship darts, Loco Beach party festival, exhibitions, family days and special events like the RAD AM RING cycling event and our most famous event, The ROCK AM RING, our rock music festival now into it's 40th year. There really is something for everyone.
